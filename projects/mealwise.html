MealWise.

Personalised nutrition, zero decision fatigue.

MealWise was designed to dissolve the daily "what's for dinner" stress for busy professionals. By combining dietary preferences with what's already in your fridge, it generates instant, healthy recipes — and gets out of the way.

Role
Lead UX & UI Designer
Timeline
8 weeks · 2025
Platform
iOS & Android
Tools
Figma · Maze
The Purpose

Making the healthy choice the easy choice.

Decision fatigue is the quiet tax on weeknight cooking. MealWise replaces an open-ended question with a small, opinionated answer: here are three meals you can actually make tonight, ranked by what you already have.

The brief was to build a flow that felt less like a recipe app and more like a thoughtful friend who happens to know what's in your fridge.
